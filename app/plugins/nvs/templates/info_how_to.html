How to Use

The Center of Digital Humanities Research, (CoDHR) is now the new home of the New Variorum Shakespeare series (NVS), taking the project over from the Modern Language Association (MLA). Texas A&M has now made the NVS editions that were only previously published in print freely available online to scholars, educators, students, and performers via a fully networked digital platform called Corpora. Visit the NVS Info / About page to learn more about the history of the NVS and the scope of this project.

Navigating the Site

Simple Search

A simple search will return a wide range of results based on keyword searching. Results are obtained by searching for terms or keywords across all fields. For example, if a user performs a simple search for the keyword “heretic” by typing the word in the Simple Search box they will get 1,003 results as the simple search returns all instances of the word in the play text as well as in the commentary notes.

An Advanced Search

An advanced search allows for a more targeted query by limiting the search to a particular play or to specific fields within and across plays on this site. Users may search by through line number, act, scene, character, date, genre, content type, and keyword.
