In 2019 the Modern Language Association’s Executive Council awarded the Center of Digital Humanities Research (CoDHR) at Texas A&M University a grant to publish NVS editions online, making them freely available to scholars, educators, students, and performers via an open access, interactive web platform. Please visit the About page to learn more about the history of the NVS and the scope of this project.

Please note that this site is in BETA mode. More work is to be done before the reader will be fully accessible to screen readers, will work seamlessly across all browsers (including mobile devices in portrait mode), and will be fully vetted in terms of the accuracy of the synthetically produced variants. At present, accessing the Variorum Reader on Google Chrome from a laptop or desktop computer provides the best experience.

To learn more about the site and how to use the Variorum Reader, please watch our instructional videos below.
